Welcome to the College of Southern Nevada
Exam Registration Portal

Secure, role-based exam booking for CSN students and faculty.

The ERS provides a simple and convenient way for CSN students to reserve testing appointments online.
Faculty are also supported through dedicated role-based features that simplify oversight and exam coordination.
Students can choose their preferred exam location, date, and time directly through the system. Once logged in, the platform automatically recognizes the user and guides them through a quick registration process. Designed to be flexible and accessible, the system allows students to view available sessions in real time, check exam availability, and manage their upcoming appointments.
