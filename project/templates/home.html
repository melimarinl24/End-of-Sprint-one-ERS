Welcome to the Exam
Registration Portal

Secure, role-based exam booking for CSN students and
faculty. Fast, simple, and reliable.

The Exam Registration System provides a simple and convenient way for CSN students to reserve testing appointments online. Instead of visiting a campus office or calling to schedule, students can choose their preferred exam location, date, and time directly through the system. Once logged in, the platform automatically recognizes the user and guides them through a quick and streamlined registration process. Designed to be flexible and accessible, the system allows students to view available sessions in real time, check exam availability, and manage their upcoming appointments; all in one place. Faculty are also supported through dedicated role-based features that simplify oversight and exam coordination.
